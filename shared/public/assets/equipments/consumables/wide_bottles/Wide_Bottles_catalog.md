### Charge System

Each potion holds **5 charges**. As charges are consumed, the liquid level visually decreases — the bottle appears progressively more "sipped" (надпитая бутылка):

- `{color}_5.png` — Full bottle (5/5 charges)
- `{color}_4.png` — Sipped once (4/5 charges)
- `{color}_3.png` — Half-spent (3/5 charges)
- `{color}_2.png` — Nearly depleted (2/5 charges)
- `{color}_1.png` — Last drop (1/5 charges)
